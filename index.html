FAQ

Questions,
answered

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach our team and we'll reply within 2 hours.

Is there a free trial?
+
Yes — every plan starts with a 14-day free trial and no credit card is required. You can try the full analytics experience before you decide.
How does pricing work for larger teams?
+
Starter and Professional plans are priced per workspace, not per seat, so costs stay predictable as your team grows. Enterprise pricing is custom based on your data volume and integration needs.
Can I connect my existing data sources?
+
Absolutely. DataVista supports Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, Postgres, and many popular cloud sources. We also offer CSV imports and API-based connections for custom pipelines.
What does the 99.9% uptime SLA mean?
+
It means DataVista is committed to less than 8.7 hours of downtime per year. We monitor availability 24/7 and provide transparency through incident reports.
Is my data secure?
+
Yes. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and our platform meets SOC 2 and GDPR standards. Enterprise plans can also include dedicated security reviews and compliance reports.
Can I cancel anytime?
+
Yes. Monthly plans can be canceled at any time. Annual plans renew automatically but can be canceled before renewal to avoid the next charge.
